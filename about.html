About Campus

Campus is a communication-first messaging app designed exclusively for teachers and students. We keep academic conversations focused, structured, and easy to manage — without mixing them with personal chats.

Our Vision

Communication, built for classrooms.

Campus gives education its own communication space, helping teachers and students stay connected without the chaos of non-academic distractions found on personal messaging apps.
